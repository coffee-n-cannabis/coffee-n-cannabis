<h1 align="center">Coffee-n-Cannabis.com</h1>

<p align="center"><em>Where code, coffee, and cannabis come together.</em></p>

---

Coffee-n-Cannabis is a few friends and colleagues with day jobs. We do security, development, engineering, and sysadmin work at different companies around the world. After hours we take on contract jobs and long-term projects of our own.

**What we do:** audits, code review, threat models, reverse engineering, incident response, software, and infrastructure.

We like working on anti-surveillance, decentralization, and zero trust, for small operators, self-hosters, federations, independent press, co-ops, and anyone building things that don't need a venture round.

We don't take work from surveillance companies.

Bring on any one of us, you have all of us. What you tell us stays here. If we can't do the work, we will say so up front.

We work across budgets, including none.

We grew up on BBSes, IRC, and Linux installs that took weekends. Most of us ended up working in the industry. So reach out and let's build something.
